Status Quo (Current Situation)

Since 1998, over 5000 victims of nodding syndrome in Northern Uganda have been suffering from seizures, severe cognitive deterioration, stunted growth, abject poverty, pains and emotional fulfilment of which minimum attention have been accorded to them. From the five districts we have a total of 4,925 survivors + 336 from Gulu + 60 for Nwoya. Which give a total of 5,321 Survivors of Nodding Disease Syndrome in Acholi Sub Region as per our Preliminary data report. Therefore, we strive to provide a healthy and positive living to the nodding syndrome victims, disabled, epileptics, elderly, and the destitute through Advocacy and commitment with partners and clients.

Quality assurance

In line with international standards, AJBOCCK quality assurance plan was developed to address crucial issues on love, pity and humanity and therefore, the organization will be keen to see that quality services are a priority in support of the Nodding Syndrome Survivors and the needy at large.

Responsive projection of AJBOCCK

In order to measure our progress in the next upcoming five years, we used SWOT Analysis which will enable AJBOCCK to implement her activities with an informed perspective of their strengths, weaknesses, threats and opportunities. AJBOCCK will employ this technique for quality delivery of her charity programs accorded to the right beneficiaries.

Strategic Direction

In accordance with the AJBOCCK’s set out vision, mission, core values, opportunities and threats in the different areas of operation, the five-year period of systematic operation will be apportioned for assessing and deepening organization’s better methodologies to all her charity activities. AJBOCCK strategic direction and goals included in this plan are; emergency response to its understanding of what the beneficiaries value most about the organization, available opportunities and challenges for offering a high quality or humanitarian system of support in the different areas of operation for particularly the most vulnerable victims within our reach.
