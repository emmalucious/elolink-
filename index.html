Mission

To stimulate, enhance and serve nodding syndrome survivors and destitute with love and compassion to survive, recover and gain control of their future through advocacy and commitment with partners and clients.

Welcome to AJBOCCK Uganda

John Baptist Odama Care Centre Kitgum, Uganda is a dully registered new Indigenous non-profit making Charity NGO situated in the Archdiocese of Gulu at Mary Immaculate of Lourdes- Kitgum Mission Parish with a given brand name as “Archbishop John Baptist Odama Care Centre Kitgum (AJBOCCK).” The organization is founded to save the lives of the nodding disease syndrome survivors and other persons of concern (i.e. the disabled, epileptics, elderly, and the destitute) in Northern Uganda (i.e. in the districts of Pader, Kitgum, Lamwo, Agago, Gulu, Omoro, Amuru, Nwoya, Lira and Oyam or any other location as may be prescribed by AJBOCCK for intervention).

Vision

To provide a healthy and positive living to the nodding syndrome survivors, disabled, epileptics, violence, destitute and elderly through advocacy and commitment with partners and clients.

Become a Partner

AJBOCCK Uganda works with all relevant stakeholders and other like-minded organizations in finding long term solutions in its quest to support and care for the nodding syndrome survivors and their famlies, epileptics, elderly and the destitute.
